## 二、三块板子

| ①备 | 电池箱 | **ESP32-S3** (DevKitC-1) | BLE Central→解析→UART TX | v1-final, GPIO17 | ✅ 备用 |

| ③ | 机械臂上 | STM32 | UART RX→PWM→舵机 | — | ✅ 联调完成 |

## 三、信号流

```
VR LOOKBON 手柄 BLE
  → ① nRF52840/ESP32-S3 BLE Central 接收 Notify
  → ① handle_parser 解析 BTN/X/Y
  → ① UART TX 发出 0xAA/dir/0xBB 帧 (115200, 3.3V)
  → ③ STM32 UART RX 接收
  → ③ STM32 生成 PWM
  → 舵机动作
```
